Usage

Include the CSS and JS files


<link rel="stylesheet" href="list-splitter/list-splitter.css" />
<script src="list-splitter/list-splitter.js"></script>

HTML markup


<ul>
	<li>Item 1</li>
	<li>Item 2</li>
	<li>Item 3</li>
	<li>Item 4</li>
	<li>Item 5</li>
	<li>Item 6</li>
	<li>Item 7</li>
	<li>Item 8</li>
	<li>Item 9</li>
	<li>Item 10</li>
</ul>

Initialize your list splitter

Where n is the number of columns you want


$('ul').listSplitter(n);

Demo

Below is an example of a list split into 3 columns.

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (1 1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 pounds chicken legs and thighs, rinsed and patted dry
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 chayote squash, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 head bok choy, chopped
  • 1/2 pound spinach
